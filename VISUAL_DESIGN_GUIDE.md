## Transitions & Animations

All interactive elements have:
- **Duration**: 150-200ms
- **Timing**: ease-out
- **Properties**: color, background, border

Examples:
- Buttons: `transition duration-150`
- Inputs: `transition` (smooth)
- Rows: `transition` (on hover)
